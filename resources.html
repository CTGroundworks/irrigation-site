Resources

Make your irrigation review more useful.

Before a visit, photos and basic observations can help narrow the issue and save time on site.

Helpful Photos to Text

Controller screen, sprinkler heads, dry spots, wet areas, runoff paths, low spots, and any proposed repair notes.

What to Notice

Which zones look weak, where water runs off, which areas stay dry, and whether issues changed after schedule adjustments.

Questions to Ask

What is the most likely cause? What should be fixed first? What can wait? Will the recommendation save water or improve coverage?
