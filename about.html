About the Owner

Central Texas Groundworks & Consulting LLC was built around hands-on field experience and a practical understanding of outdoor properties in Central Texas. The goal is to help property owners make smarter decisions about drainage, landscapes, water use, site conditions, and property improvements.

Every property is different. Recommendations should be based on what is actually happening on the site, not a one-size-fits-all sales package. The focus is honest communication, practical options, careful work, and solutions that make sense for the property and the customer.
