Hands-On Experience Behind Every Recommendation

Central Texas Groundworks & Consulting LLC is an owner-operated business built around hands-on field experience with turf, landscapes, drainage conditions, soil, slopes, and property maintenance in demanding Central Texas environments.

That field experience developed a practical understanding of how clay soil, intense heat, runoff, plant placement, water movement, maintenance practices, and site conditions affect a property over time.

The goal is not to sell every customer the same package or claim that every problem has a simple fix. The goal is to look carefully at what is happening, explain realistic options, define a clear scope, and complete work that fits the property, priorities, access, and budget.
