About

A knowledgeable irrigation specialist, not a giant landscape company.

Central Texas Groundworks & Consulting LLC helps homeowners make better decisions about irrigation performance, water efficiency, and outdoor water movement.

Practical guidance before expensive repairs.

The focus is simple: understand what is actually limiting the system, explain the issue clearly, and recommend the next best step. Sometimes that means controller changes. Sometimes it means a nozzle, pressure, coverage, drainage, or scheduling issue. Sometimes it means avoiding an upgrade that will not solve the real problem.

Central Texas conditions matter. Clay soil, slope, runoff, compacted ground, heat stress, mixed sun exposure, and watering rules can make a generic irrigation schedule waste water or leave parts of the yard struggling.

What You Can Expect

  • Clear explanations without pressure
  • Central Texas-specific recommendations
  • Residential-friendly communication
  • Focus on performance, efficiency, and landscape health
