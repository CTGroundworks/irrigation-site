Our Story

Central Texas Groundworks & Consulting LLC was founded by DJ, an irrigation specialist and greenskeeper based in Dallas, Texas. What began as a passion for tinkering with sprinkler systems and nurturing healthy turf has grown into a full‑service consulting firm. We specialize in land clearing, site preparation, irrigation design and water‑wise landscape management. With a background in agronomy and a love for technology, DJ set out to offer practical, science‑backed solutions for homeowners, property managers and municipalities across Central Texas.

Our Approach

We believe that every landscape can be both beautiful and sustainable. By combining thorough site assessments with data‑driven recommendations, we help clients make informed decisions. Our approach emphasizes efficient water use, proper plant selection, soil improvement and proactive maintenance to reduce long‑term costs and environmental impact.

Meet the Founder

DJ balances multiple ventures—from irrigation consulting and pressure washing services to creative projects like Ninja Critters. Outside of work, he enjoys DIY projects, camping and spending time with family. This diversity of experience informs our flexible, client‑centered service philosophy. Whether you need an irrigation audit, help diagnosing a turf disease or guidance on starting your own landscape project, we’re here to support you.
