Hands-On Experience Behind Every Project

Central Texas Groundworks & Consulting LLC is owner-operated by Garth Nixon, a Certified Irrigation Technician with approximately 10 years of hands-on experience managing irrigation, turf, landscapes, and outdoor property conditions at a Central Texas golf course.

That work involved much more than keeping grass green. It included managing irrigation performance across changing elevations and soil conditions, completing landscape projects, building and improving planting beds, addressing drainage and runoff problems, controlling erosion, installing and maintaining landscape rock, troubleshooting difficult turf areas, and helping outdoor spaces perform under intense Central Texas heat and drought conditions.

Garth started Central Texas Groundworks & Consulting to bring that same practical, detail-focused approach to homeowners, property managers, HOAs, and small commercial properties. He enjoys taking an outdoor space that is struggling, unfinished, or difficult to maintain and helping turn it into something healthier, more functional, and more visually appealing.

The goal is to listen to what the property owner wants, understand what the site will realistically support, and bring those improvements to life with clear communication, careful planning, and hands-on work.

I have always enjoyed bringing landscape improvements and outdoor spaces to life. After years of doing that work in a demanding golf-course environment, I wanted to bring the same care, problem-solving, and attention to detail to homeowners and properties throughout Central Texas.

How the work is approached

Experience You Can See in the Process

Direct Owner Involvement

You work directly with the owner reviewing the property, defining the scope, and completing or overseeing the work.

Field-Based Recommendations

Recommendations are based on real site conditions, including soil, slopes, drainage, turf, plants, access, and long-term maintenance needs.

Clear Project Scope

The quote explains what is included, what is not included, and what conditions could affect the work or cost.

Honest Capability Limits

Projects requiring specialized licensing, engineering, heavy equipment, major hauling, or outside trades are identified before the work is accepted.

Real Project Documentation

When appropriate, photos document the existing conditions, preparation, work completed, and areas that may need future attention.
