Services

Irrigation consulting, troubleshooting, and practical water-use guidance.

Focused support for homeowners who want to understand the issue before spending money on the wrong repair.

Irrigation System Reviews

Review sprinkler coverage, zone performance, visible head and nozzle issues, pressure concerns, controller settings, runoff, and dry areas.

Sprinkler Troubleshooting

Identify likely causes of poor coverage, weak zones, blocked heads, mismatched nozzles, sunken heads, and uneven watering.

Controller Programming

Adjust watering schedules for season, soil, slope, turf needs, watering restrictions, and actual zone performance.

Cycle and Soak Planning

Plan shorter repeat cycles where clay soil or slope causes runoff before water has time to soak into the root zone.

Water-Efficiency Consulting

Find practical ways to reduce waste, improve soak-in, avoid unnecessary run time, and support healthier turf and landscape areas.

Drainage and Water Movement

Review visible water movement, low spots, slope, runoff paths, saturated areas, and outdoor water concerns.

Landscape and Turf Support

Support irrigation-related turf stress, dry spots, shallow watering issues, compacted areas, and uneven soil moisture.

Second Opinions

Review proposed repairs or upgrades before you spend money on larger work.
