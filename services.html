We offer a variety of services designed to keep your irrigation system running efficiently and your landscape thriving. Whether you need a one‑time evaluation or ongoing support, our team is ready to help.

Irrigation System Review

We inspect your existing irrigation system, evaluate performance and identify inefficiencies, dry spots and run‑off issues. You receive a detailed report and prioritized recommendations.

Controller Programming & Optimization

Our experts program controllers and smart timers to align watering schedules with plant needs, local weather patterns and regulatory guidelines, ensuring water is used effectively.

Water Efficiency Consulting

We analyze your site’s water use and develop strategies to reduce waste without sacrificing plant health. Recommendations may include nozzle upgrades, pressure regulation and scheduling adjustments.

Dry Spot & Runoff Troubleshooting

Localized dry areas and runoff can indicate uneven coverage or compaction. We diagnose the underlying cause, adjust heads, amend soil and implement cycle‑and‑soak programming.

Agronomic & Turf Health Support

Beyond watering, we provide guidance on mowing height, fertilizer selection, disease management and species selection to keep your lawn lush and resilient.

Drainage & Water Movement Assessment

Poor drainage leads to standing water and root disease. We evaluate grading and soil structure, recommend French drains or swales and improve soil porosity through aeration and compost topdressing.
