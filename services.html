Services

Practical property services for difficult Central Texas sites.

Clear outdoor property work for homeowners, property managers, HOAs, and small commercial properties dealing with clay soil, slopes, runoff, heat, turf, planting areas, cleanup, and site access challenges.

01

Property & Landscape Evaluations

Practical reviews of visible site conditions, turf, planting areas, drainage, runoff, soil, slopes, sunlight, access, and realistic improvement opportunities.

02

Drainage & Runoff Observations

Identify visible standing water, erosion, downspout discharge concerns, blocked or damaged landscape drain lines, poor discharge locations, and slope runoff.

03

Sod Installation & Lawn Renovation

Remove damaged turf, prepare the planting surface, make targeted soil improvements, handle minor grade correction, and install sod suited to the conditions.

04

Planting & Landscape-Bed Improvements

Plant installation, bed preparation, mulch, decorative rock, edging, soil improvement, plant replacement, and small landscape-bed renovations.

05

Water, Soil & Slope Observations

Surface-level recommendations for compacted soil, poor infiltration, runoff, visible dry areas, plant stress, slope conditions, and difficult growing environments.

06

Property Cleanup & Light Site Preparation

Vegetation cleanup, dead plant material removal, debris consolidation, planting-area preparation, small-area clearing, and prep for sod, beds, drainage cleanup, or other outdoor work.

07

Additional Service: Pressure Washing

Pressure washing may be available for selected driveways, sidewalks, patios, and exterior surfaces after surface condition, water access, plants, and runoff risks are reviewed.

Project fit

Some Work Is Quoted Only After Scope and Access Are Reviewed

Small-area clearing, hauling, equipment-assisted cleanup, and minor grading may be available when the property, access, rental needs, disposal needs, project size, and schedule make the work practical. Heavy excavation, large-scale clearing, engineered plans, and licensed specialty work are not advertised as standard services.
