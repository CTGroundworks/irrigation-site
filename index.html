Welcome

Central Texas Groundworks & Consulting LLC offers comprehensive irrigation design, troubleshooting, and turf health services for homeowners, businesses, and property managers across Central Texas. Based in Leander, we serve communities including Leander, Liberty Hill, Cedar Park, Georgetown, Round Rock, Lago Vista, Lakeway, Bee Cave, and nearby Hill Country areas. We combine practical field experience with science‑based recommendations to help you save water, maintain beautiful landscapes, and protect your investment.
