Our Main Services

Landscape Planning & Property Evaluations

Practical property reviews focused on layout, plant health, drainage, site conditions, and long-term improvement opportunities.

Drainage & Runoff Assessments

Evaluate water movement, erosion, standing water, downspout discharge, slope runoff, and problem areas around the property.

Irrigation Review

Review visible irrigation performance, coverage concerns, watering practices, pressure symptoms, runoff, and landscape water use.

Sod Installation & Lawn Renovation

Remove damaged turf, improve the soil where needed, prepare the site, and install sod suited to the property conditions.

Plant & Landscape-Bed Installation

Install plants, landscape beds, edging, mulch, rock, and practical improvements based on sunlight, drainage, soil, and maintenance needs.

Property Cleanup & Site Preparation

Clear unwanted vegetation, remove debris, prepare planting areas, improve access, and get outdoor spaces ready for the next phase of work.

Water-Management Guidance

Provide practical recommendations for reducing runoff, improving watering efficiency, protecting slopes, and supporting healthier landscapes.

Additional Service: Pressure Washing

Exterior surface cleaning may be available as an additional service for selected residential and small commercial projects.

Why Central Texas Properties Need Specialized Solutions

Central Texas properties face difficult conditions that generic landscaping recommendations often overlook. Every project should account for the actual soil, grade, water movement, sunlight, plant needs, and long-term maintenance requirements of the property.

About the Owner

Central Texas Groundworks & Consulting LLC was built around hands-on field experience and a practical understanding of outdoor properties in Central Texas. The goal is to help property owners make smarter decisions about drainage, landscapes, water use, site conditions, and property improvements.

Every property is different. Recommendations should be based on what is actually happening on the site, not a one-size-fits-all sales package. The focus is honest communication, practical options, careful work, and solutions that make sense for the property and the customer.
